Python is my favourite language. Not only was python easy to learn, but also its readablity and cross platform compatiability was very appealing to me. Raspberry Pi

Several Projects and tutorials at Perleybrook Labs



Raspberry pi opened up a whole new world. In fact, I learned python and linux commands so that i can work with the raspberry pi. Having a full fletched linux machine gives us freedom to do a lot more than a microcontroller. The ability of the Rpi to connect to the network , gave me opportunity to work with networks and work with concepts like "Internet of things" and also helped me understand networking concepts. I got the opportunity to work with the platform at my current work place ,Perleybrook Labs LLC. I could do a lot of work on the raspberry Pi and learn a lot from them , all thanks to My mentor Mr Ajith Peter and my Employer Mr Ranjith Antony.

Touche

Geture recoganising Capacitive touch sensing



Touche is a touch-gusture recoganition technology from Disney Laboratory,Pittsburg. Touche can recoganise not just the location of the touch but also the nature of touch. There was a tutorial in the net that emulates the same technique with an arduino. I implemented and presented this at a project expo. and the next step was to implement the same with a raspberry pi. Unfortunately the project was not documented to be shown here, but was a very unique and pragatic concept and one of my personal favorites and I taught that was an honourable mention here.
